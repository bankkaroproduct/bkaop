Diagnostic: Rail-Led to Platform-Led

Dimension Before (Rail-Led) Now (Platform-Led)
Product No standalone product. Embedded in CK/EK. Great.cards (web) + Dr.BankKaro (app). Owned surfaces.
Distribution Borrowed CK/EK traffic. No owned channels. Owned: organic SEO, influencers, whitelabel, API, app download, corporate
Revenue Tracking Bundled with CK/EK P&L. No separate attribution. 10 streams with per-stream unit economics and clarity
GTM Ownership Shared team, no dedicated product/ops focus Dedicated product team + ops. Clear ownership model.
Reusability One-off integrations per partner Whitelabel in 30 mins. API for any partner. Corporate for employers.
User Relationship None. One-time visitor, no engagement layer Dr.BankKaro creates retained, daily-active users. Recurring subscription revenue.

The shift from rail-dependent to platform-led is structurally complete. Banking monetization no longer depends on CK/EK distribution or product leverage. The platform now owns its product identity, distribution channels, and user relationship. This unlocks independent unit economics, repeatable commercialization (whitelabel, API, corporate), and a path to recurring revenue (Dr.BankKaro subscription). Historical numbers are weak because the infrastructure was not designed as a standalone business. That has changed.
